UP & DOWN

Predict the trend, raise your stakes, and win.

UP & DOWN is a decentralized, high-fidelity prediction platform built on the Chiliz Chain. It allows you to capitalize on the price movements of CHZ in real time.

What is UP & DOWN?
Predict whether the CHZ price will go UP or DOWN within a 10 minute round. If your prediction is correct when the round closes, you share the prize pool with other winners.

📋 Summary: How It Works

A step by step walkthrough of the prediction flow.

  1. UP & DOWN exclusively supports the CHZ/USD pair.
  2. Predict if the CHZ price will be higher (UP) or lower (DOWN) than the Locked Price when the 10 minute round concludes.
  3. Enter your desired amount of CHZ.
  4. Once your entry is submitted for a "LIVE" round, it cannot be edited, cancelled, or removed.
  5. Win or lose:
    • UP Wins: If Closed Price > Locked Price at the end of the round.
    • DOWN Wins: If Closed Price < Locked Price at the end of the round.

⚙️ Mechanics & Fees

Technical details on rounds, fees, and payout calculations.

  • Blockchain: Powered by Chiliz Chain (Mainnet).
  • Round Frequency: Rolling rounds occur every 10 minutes.
  • Platform Fee: A small portion of the total prize pool is allocated to $FAN. These funds are dedicated to continuous platform development and are also subject to buyback and burn.
  • Winnings: Users can claim their rewards immediately after a round is finalized and on-chain data is verified.

Payout Calculation

Payouts are based on the ratio of bets in each pool:

Payout Ratio (UP Pool) = Total value of both pools ÷ Value of UP Pool
Payout Ratio (DOWN Pool) = Total value of both pools ÷ Value of DOWN Pool

⚖️ Outcomes & Special Rules

What happens when a round closes.

  • Victory: Share the total prize pool with fellow winners.
  • Loss: Your entry is forfeited to the winning side of the pool.
  • Tie (Locked Price = Closed Price): In the rare event of an exact price match, the house retains all entries.
  • Single-Sided Betting: If there are no opposing bets, winners reclaim their initial entry minus the service fee.
  • System Cancellation: If an Oracle error occurs, all participants receive a 100% refund of their initial entry.

🔮 Price Feeds (Oracle)

How UP & DOWN determines the price of CHZ each round.

UP & DOWN prioritizes accuracy and speed by utilizing Pyth Network as its primary data source.

Chain Market Purpose Oracle
Chiliz Chain CHZ/USD Sets the Lock Price and Closed Price (updated ~ up to 20 seconds) Pyth Network

Why Pyth Network?

Unlike traditional oracles, Pyth provides sub-second latency and institutional-grade data directly from first-party financial sources. This ensures that the prices used for settlement are transparent, manipulation-resistant, and reflect the true global market value of CHZ at the exact moment a round closes.
